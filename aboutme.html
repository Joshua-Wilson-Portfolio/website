Overview

Hi, I'm Joshua Wilson — a data analyst with a strong foundation in statistics, a passion for problem-solving, and a sharp eye for turning raw data into actionable insights.

After completing a BSc in Mathematics with Statistics from Newcastle University, I spent several years in sales and operational roles, where I naturally gravitated toward data-driven decision-making. That hands-on experience, combined with recent training in data science and tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, has given me a unique blend of commercial awareness and analytical skill.

This GitHub portfolio showcases some of my projects — from data cleaning scripts to interactive dashboards — all aimed at driving business performance through data-led decisions.
