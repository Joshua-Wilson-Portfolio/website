About Me

Hi, I'm Joshua Wilson — a data analyst with a strong foundation in statistics, a passion for problem-solving, and a sharp eye for turning raw data into actionable insights.

After completing a BSc in Mathematics with Statistics from Newcastle University, I spent several years in sales and operational roles, where I naturally gravitated toward data-driven decision-making.

That hands-on experience, combined with recent training in data science and tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Excel, has given me a unique blend of commercial awareness and analytical skill.

I’ve created this GitHub portfolio to showcase some of my analytics projects — from data cleaning scripts in Python / SQL to interactive dashboards on Power BI / Tableau that support business performance.

I’m currently open to analyst opportunities where I can bring data to life and contribute to impactful decisions.
