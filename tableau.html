Tableau Dashboards

This project showcases Tableau dashboards created using real-world call centre and sales datasets. These dashboards provide visual insights into key performance indicators, agent activity, customer experience, and product performance.

  • Dynamic filters for region, agent, and date ranges
  • Custom KPIs for call resolution times and customer satisfaction
  • Interactive elements such as tooltips, highlights, and drilldowns

These dashboards were designed to support operational managers and sales teams in identifying trends and performance gaps, enabling timely and effective decision-making.
